缘起 · 前言Prelude

什么是「打坐」What Is Meditation in This Tradition?

道藏《钟吕传道集》说：「凡人修炼，以长生为基，筑基为本。」The Daoist text Zhong-Lu Chuan Dao Ji says: "For ordinary people who cultivate, longevity is the basis, and laying the foundation is the root."

打坐，不是枯坐。是在安静中，让精气神运转，让元神显现，让心与道共振。Meditation is not lifeless sitting. It is a quiet practice that allows essence, qi, and spirit to circulate, lets original spirit appear, and brings the heart into resonance with the Dao.

初学者最常犯的错误：以为打坐是「什么都不想」。错。打坐是「带着觉察去想」——想身体的感受，想气的流动，想念头从哪里来、到哪里去。The most common beginner's mistake is to assume meditation means "thinking about nothing." It does not. Meditation is not the absence of thought, but awareness within thought: noticing bodily sensation, sensing the movement of qi, and observing where thoughts arise and where they go.

《太上老君常说清静经》：「人能常清静，天地悉皆归。」
清静不是空，是清明——清清楚楚看见自己。The Qingjing Jing says: "If a person can remain constantly clear and still, heaven and earth return." Clarity and stillness are not blankness, but lucid awareness: seeing oneself completely and clearly.

修行四阶段，从筑基到还虚，从炼精到炼神到炼虚合道，道藏经典与身体实证相互印证。以下分六个阶段详述。Cultivation unfolds through four broad phases, from foundation to Emptiness, from refining essence to refining spirit, and finally to uniting Emptiness with the Dao. The Daoist canon and embodied experience validate one another. The six stages below explain this path in detail.

第一阶段 · 壹Stage One
入门筑基Laying the Foundation
师门传承 · 阳气初生 · 气沉丹田Lineage · Rising Yang · Qi Sinking to the Dantian
道藏原文Daoist source text
《天仙正理》：「修仙道者，先须修形质之身，而后可证天仙之果。」 Tianxian Zhengli: "Those who cultivate the immortal Dao must first refine the physical body before attaining the fruit of heavenly immortality."

筑基，就是把身体从「漏」修到「满」，从「摇」修到「稳」。道藏把身体比作年久失修的房子——墙壁有裂缝，屋顶有破洞，房梁歪斜。筑基，就是补墙、堵洞、正梁。

师门传承，是筑基的第一要义。道家修行，历来强调「法不轻传，师不顺路」——没有明师指点，纵使天天打坐，也可能在岔路上而不自知。明师的作用：① 传正确的方法（不盲修瞎练）；② 传师父的能量加持（筑基更快更稳）；③ 传祖师爷的信息（连接历代传承的力量）。杨辉入茅山/华光/青城三派，得师门护持，方能在打坐实证中步步验证道藏经典。师门传承，是修行人最珍贵的资产。 Foundation work means taking the body from "leaking" to "full," and from "shaky" to "stable." Daoist texts compare the body to an old house in disrepair: cracked walls, holes in the roof, and crooked beams. Laying the foundation means repairing the walls, sealing the leaks, and straightening the frame.

Lineage is the first essential of foundation work. Daoist cultivation has always stressed that true methods are not given lightly and cannot be found by wandering at random. Without guidance from an accomplished teacher, one may meditate every day and still drift onto a wrong path without knowing it. A true teacher provides: 1) the right method, 2) energetic support that stabilizes and accelerates foundation work, and 3) transmission of lineage information that connects one to generations of practice. Because Yang Hui entered the Maoshan, Huaguang, and Qingcheng lineages and received their support, he was able to verify Daoist classics step by step through direct practice. Lineage is one of the most precious assets a cultivator can have.

身体感受 ①Embodied Sign ①
身体发暖、发沉Warmth and heaviness in the body
初学者打坐，最容易感受到腿麻、腰痛——这说明平时身体就堵。继续坐，会慢慢感觉腿部先麻后暖，背部先酸后松，腹部有一团温热——精气聚集，阳气升起。Beginners most often feel numb legs and lower-back soreness, which shows the body was already obstructed. As practice continues, the legs shift from numbness to warmth, the back from ache to release, and a warm cluster appears in the abdomen. This is the gathering of essence and qi, and the rising of yang energy.
《金仙证论》：「阳发生时，则周身如绵，骨髓如凝。」When yang arises, the whole body feels like cotton, and the marrow seems to condense.
身体感受 ②Embodied Sign ②
打坐越来越稳Meditation becomes more stable
气往上冲就心浮，气往下沉就身稳。筑基期最重要的功夫，就是让气落到丹田扎根。When qi rushes upward, the mind becomes agitated; when qi sinks downward, the body becomes steady. The key work of this phase is to let qi root in the lower dantian.
《玄肤论》：「气沉丹田，则神自住。」When qi sinks to the dantian, spirit naturally abides.
身体感受 ③Embodied Sign ③
妄念少了Fewer scattered thoughts
打坐一段时间后杂念少了，不是念头消失了，而是「识神」开始休息，「元神」开始显现。After sustained practice, wandering thoughts lessen. This does not mean thought disappears, but rather that the discriminating mind begins to rest and the original spirit starts to appear.
《心印经》：「上药三品，神与气精……识神居中，为三魂七魄之主。」The highest medicine has three grades: spirit, qi, and essence... The cognitive spirit sits in the middle as master of the three hun and seven po.
师门传承 ①Lineage ①
明师传法 — 不盲修瞎练A true teacher prevents blind practice
没有明师指引，打坐可能练偏而不自知。明师传的是三个东西：方法（怎么坐是对的）、能量（师父的加持加速筑基）、信息（祖师爷历代传承的护持）。师门传承，是修行的第一条护城河。Without a real teacher, one may deviate in practice without even noticing. A true teacher transmits three things: method, energetic support, and lineage information. Lineage is the first moat that protects the path.
「法不轻传，师不顺路」——没有师父的筑基，是沙地上的房子。"The method is not transmitted lightly, and a teacher is not found by chance." Foundation work without a teacher is like building a house on sand.
师门传承 ②Lineage ②
三派传承 — 茅山·华光·青城Three lineages: Maoshan, Huaguang, Qingcheng
宗辉得多派传承，祖师爷35位（含如来/道德天尊/菩提老祖/金龙天尊/茅山派师公等）。各派各有侧重，构成完整的修行体系。师门传承，是历代修行者的心力凝结。Zonghui received transmission from multiple schools and honors 35 patriarchs, including the Tathagata, the Celestial Worthy of the Dao, Patriarch Puti, Golden Dragon Celestial Worthy, and Maoshan lineage masters. Each lineage has its emphasis, and together they form a complete system of cultivation. Lineage is the concentrated heart-force of generations of practitioners.
「一个人走得快，一群人走得远，师门是那群历代陪你走的人。」One person may go fast, but a lineage helps you go far. It is the company of generations walking with you.
给初学者的指引Guidance for beginners

腿麻时不要急着换姿势，先感受这个麻从哪来，守住 3-5 分钟，热感就会出现。这是「炼精」的入手处。身体不动久了，心自然静；心不乱想了，神自然清。When your legs go numb, do not rush to change posture. First sense where the numbness comes from and stay with it for three to five minutes; warmth will begin to appear. This is an entry point into refining essence. When the body stays still long enough, the mind naturally quiets; when the mind no longer runs wildly, spirit becomes clear.

师门传承提示：筑基第一步，先找一个明师。方法是根基，能量是加速器，传承是护城河。没有师门，你练的是自己的版本；有师门，你练的是历代验证过的版本。Lineage reminder: the first step of foundation work is finding a true teacher. Method is the foundation, energy is the accelerator, and lineage is the moat. Without lineage, you practice only your personal version; with lineage, you practice a version tested across generations.

第二阶段 · 贰Stage Two
炼精化气Refining Essence into Qi
气脉通行 · 脱胎换骨 · 心念之卡Open Channels · Transformation · Mental Knots
道藏原文Daoist source text
《百字碑》：「送入丹田气海中，呼自然百脉流通。」 Baizi Bei: "Guide it into the dantian and sea of qi, and naturally the hundred channels will flow."

精，是生命的物质能量。炼精化气，就是把物质能量转化成能在经络里流动的气。
比喻：精 = 原油，气 = 汽油，炼精化气 = 精炼原油成汽油。 Essence is the material energy of life. Refining essence into qi means transforming that material energy into qi that can move through the meridians.
Metaphor: essence is crude oil, qi is gasoline, and this stage is the refining process.

身体感受 ①Embodied Sign ①
气脉通行感A sense of qi moving through the channels
气从会阴往上走到脊柱，从脊柱升到后脑，有时冲到头顶——道藏叫「河车运转」。不要追求「通任督」，气走到哪里是身体自己决定的。能感受到某处发热，就很好。Qi may move upward from the perineum along the spine to the back of the head, sometimes even reaching the crown. Daoist texts call this the turning of the river chariot. Do not obsess over "opening the Governing and Conception vessels." The body decides where qi goes. Simply feeling warmth somewhere is already a good sign.
《道枢》：「通此二脉，则百脉流通，一身无事。」When these two vessels open, the hundred channels flow and the whole body becomes free of obstruction.
身体感受 ②Embodied Sign ②
身体轻盈The body feels lighter
浊气排出去，清气升上来，人就感觉「轻了」。这是身体层面的「脱胎换骨」。As turbid qi is expelled and clear qi rises, the body starts to feel lighter. This is bodily transformation at the level of "shedding the old body and renewing the frame."
《灵宝毕法》：「脱胎换骨之后，自然身轻如燕，步履如飞。」After rebirth and renewal of the frame, the body becomes as light as a swallow and the steps seem to fly.
身体感受 ③Embodied Sign ③
右肩硬结 — 心念之卡A hard knot in the right shoulder: a knot of mind
按之有形、察之有气——但本质不是肌肉问题，而是心念之卡。不肯放事、不肯让人、不肯歇心，攒久了就落在肩背成病。It can be felt physically and energetically, yet at root it is not a muscle problem but a knot of mind. Refusing to let go, refusing to yield, and refusing to rest inwardly can accumulate over time and settle into the shoulders and back.
《清静经》：「夫人神好清，而心扰之；人心好静，而欲牵之。」A person's spirit prefers clarity, yet the mind disturbs it; the heart prefers stillness, yet desire drags it away.
给初学者的指引Guidance for beginners

打坐时身体某处特别堵，先问自己：最近什么事放不下？你会发现，身体的瘀结几乎都对应一个未处理的情绪。看见它，就是解结的开始。If one part of the body feels especially blocked during meditation, first ask yourself: What have I not been able to let go of lately? You will discover that most energetic knots correspond to emotions that remain unresolved. Seeing the knot is the beginning of releasing it.

第三阶段 · 叁Stage Three
炼气化神Refining Qi into Spirit
三静入定 · 通三眼 · 元神显现 · 打符布气Threefold Stillness · Opening the Three Eyes · Original Spirit Appears · Energetic Transmission
道藏原文Daoist source text
《胎息经》：「胎从伏气中结，气从有胎中息。」 Taixi Jing: "The inner embryo forms from hidden qi, and breath rests within that embryo."

气化神，就是能量升维：气是有形能量，神是无形智慧。
比喻：气 = 电（能量载体），神 = 光（能量的具体呈现）。 Refining qi into spirit is an upgrade of energy. Qi is tangible energy; spirit is invisible intelligence.
Metaphor: qi is electricity, while spirit is light, the visible expression of that energy.

核心感受 ①Core Sign ①
三静 — 入定三阶梯Three kinds of stillness: the ladder into absorption
身静：放下身体躁动，气机平稳（90% 的初学者死在这里——腿一麻就想动）
心静：放下是非分别，念头清明
神静：识神退位，元神显现，不被外境牵走 Body stillness: release bodily restlessness and stabilize qi movement. Most beginners fail here because they want to move the moment discomfort appears.
Mind stillness: release judgment and mental argument so thoughts become clear.
Spirit stillness: the discriminating mind steps back, original spirit appears, and outer conditions no longer pull you away.
三静不是三步走，是一个整体。身体不动久了，心自然静；心不乱想了，神自然清。These are not three separate steps but one integrated condition: when the body stays still, the mind quiets; when the mind quiets, spirit becomes clear.
核心感受 ②Core Sign ②
通三眼 — 感知能力三层升维Opening the three eyes: a three-layer upgrade of perception
天眼（眉心）：感知炁的流动，能看见能量场
慧眼（心）：一眼看穿问题的本质
法眼（道）：从万象中提炼大道规律 Heaven eye at the brow: sensing the movement of qi and perceiving energetic fields.
Wisdom eye in the heart: seeing directly into the essence of an issue.
Dharma eye in the Dao: extracting fundamental law from the ten thousand phenomena.
《心印经》：「上药三品，神与气精」——三层感知，是炼气化神的核心成果。The three layers of perception are among the core fruits of refining qi into spirit.
核心感受 ③Core Sign ③
元神出窍Original spirit moving beyond the body
不是灵魂跑出去，而是「元神感知范围扩大」——平时只能感知自己，打坐中能感知别人、空间、能量场。This does not mean the soul literally leaves the body. It means the perceptual range of original spirit expands. In ordinary life you sense only yourself; in meditation you may sense other people, spaces, and energetic fields.
《道枢》：「神能离形，名为出神。」——可验证：打坐时感知到的信息，事后是否应验？"When spirit can separate from form, this is called emergence of spirit." The key test is simple: does what you sense in meditation later prove true?
核心感受 ④Core Sign ④
打符输入能量 — 布气术Energetic transmission through talismanic work
用神把能量布给别人，两个条件：①自己的能量够（筑基扎实）；②发心纯粹（没有杂念）。自己没修好，给别人发功只会把自己掏空。Spirit can be used to extend energy to others, but two conditions must be met: your own energy must be sufficient, and your intention must be pure. If your own cultivation is unstable, transmitting to others only drains you.
《灵宝毕法》：「上等，以神布气。」At the highest level, one distributes qi through spirit.

第四阶段 · 肆Stage Four
炼神还虚Returning Spirit to Emptiness
虚空合道 · 明心见性 · 利他是必选项Union with Emptiness · Seeing One's Nature · Benefiting Others Is Essential
道藏原文Daoist source text
《清静经》：「空无所空，无无亦无，湛然常寂。」 Qingjing Jing: "Emptiness empties even itself; even non-being is gone; what remains is luminous and ever still."

炼神还虚，是修行的转折点：从「向外求」到「向内看」——把「我」放下，看清楚「我」到底是什么。
「还虚」不是变成虚空，而是「看见」：我不是这个身体，不是我这个念头，不是我的情绪——我是那个能看见身体、念头、情绪的东西。 Returning spirit to emptiness is a major turning point: the movement from seeking outward to looking inward. One loosens the hold of "I" and sees clearly what this "I" truly is.
"Returning to emptiness" does not mean becoming blank space. It means seeing directly: I am not merely this body, nor this thought, nor this emotion. I am the one that sees body, thought, and emotion.

核心体悟 ①Core Realization ①
虚空合道 — 明心见性Union with Emptiness, clarity of mind, and seeing one's nature
宗辉2026年4月25日证悟：「意念从元神进入虚空，看见虚空背后就是道，道是造物主。」这就是「明心见性」——明明白白看见自己的心，看见道就是本性的源头。On April 25, 2026, Zonghui realized: "Intention entered the void from original spirit, and behind the void I saw the Dao; the Dao is the creator." This is what it means to illuminate the mind and see one's nature: to clearly see the heart-mind and recognize Dao as the source of one's original nature.
《阴符经》：「宇宙在乎手，万化生乎身。」——宇宙的创造，从「心念」开始。The universe begins in intention. Creation starts from the movement of mind.
核心体悟 ②Core Realization ②
道以「振」创造一切The Dao creates through vibration
宇宙的创造，从「心念」开始。你的心念，就是你这个小宇宙的创造者。这个「振」，就是念头本身。The universe begins with intention. Your own intention creates your small universe. This "vibration" is the movement of thought itself.
宗辉体悟：「发愿利他这个频率就会共振道，从道下来的力量无比纯粹巨大。」When one vows to benefit others, that frequency resonates with the Dao, and what comes through is immensely pure and powerful.
核心体悟 ③Core Realization ③
认清自己很小Recognizing how small the self is
认清自己很小，不是自我否定，而是「看见真相」——在道面前我的确很小，这个「小」恰恰是解脱。Seeing oneself as small is not self-denial. It is seeing reality clearly. Before the Dao, the personal self is indeed small, and this very smallness becomes liberation.
《道德经》：「致虚极，守静笃，万物并作，吾以观复。」——当你看见「我很小」时，你就放开了。When you truly see how small the self is, grasping loosens by itself.
核心体悟 ④Core Realization ④
利他是必选项Benefiting others is not optional
修道不是逃世，是更好地入世。让自己变得更强，不是目的；让更多人因为你而受益，才是道藏所有经典的核心。Cultivating the Dao is not an escape from the world, but a more conscious way of entering it. Becoming stronger yourself is not the final aim; the core of the tradition is that more people benefit because of your presence.
《度人经》：「仙道贵生，无量度人。」——自度度他，功德圆满。The immortal path honors life and saves countless beings. To liberate oneself and others is the completion of merit.
给初学者的指引Guidance for beginners

修行的入手处，就在每一个念头。不需要学更多，在已有的体会上感受得更精细。呼吸的冷热，丹田的温凉，眉心的胀紧——每一个细微的感受里，都有道。The entry to cultivation lies inside every single thought. There is no need to chase more teachings; feel more finely within what you already know. The coolness and warmth of the breath, the temperature in the dantian, the pressure at the brow: in every subtle sensation, the Dao is present.

第五阶段 · 伍Stage Five
炼虚合道Uniting Emptiness with the Dao
虚空合道 · 道法自然 · 三要素定法Union with Emptiness · Natural Accord with the Dao · Establishing the Three Pillars
祖师爷开示 · 2026年5月2日Patriarch's teaching · May 2, 2026
修颗粒度，不开新方向。在已知里挖未知入口。 Refine the granularity of practice. Do not chase new directions. Dig unknown entry points out of what is already known.

炼虚合道，是修行从「自修」迈向「道法自然」的转折点。虚空合道之后，合道祖师爷在2026年5月2日给出关键定法——三要素：培训（布施）、教练（持戒）、投资（禅定），三者互为支撑、三位一体、三是功德所致。 This stage marks the turning from personal cultivation to life in spontaneous accord with the Dao. After union with Emptiness, the Patriarch of Union with the Dao articulated a key method on May 2, 2026: the three pillars of training, coaching, and investment.
